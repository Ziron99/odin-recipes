Lasagna


Description

A dish to satisfy everyone in the family! This is a top-rated easy lasagna recipe that comes together quickly with a relatively short list of ingredients


Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1(32 ounce) jar spaghetti sauce
  • 32 ounces shredded mozzarella cheese, devided
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 teaspoons dried parsley
  • salt to taste
  • ground black pepper to taste
  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1/2 cup water

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients and preheat the oven to 350 degrees F(175 degrees C)
  2. Heat large skillet over medium-high heat.
  3. Cook and stir ground beef in the hot skillet until browned and crumbly(8 - 10 minutes)
  4. Drain and discard grease
  5. Stir in spaghetti sauce and simmer for 5 minutes
  6. Combine cottage cheese, 2 cups of mozzarella cheese, eggs, 1/2 of the grated Parmesan cheese, dried parsley, salt and pepper in a large bowl
  7. Spread 3/4 cup of sauce in a 9x13-inch baking dish
  8. Cover with 3 uncooked lasagna noodles, 1 3/4 cups of cheese mixture and 1/4 cup sauce; repeat layers once more
  9. Top with remaining 3 noodles, sauce, mozzarella and Parmesan cheese
  10. Pour 1/2 cup water along the edges of the dish
  11. Cover tightly with aluminum foil
  12. Bake in the preheated oven for 45 min
  13. Uncover and bake for aditional 10 min and let stand 10 min before serving
