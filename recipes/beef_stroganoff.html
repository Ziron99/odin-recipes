Beef Stroganoff


Description

This rich and creamy beef stroganoff recipe is made with ground beef and served with egg noodles. It's super simple to prepare and ready to serve in under 30 minutes making it a great choice for busy weeknights.


Ingredients

  • 1(8 ounce) package egg noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1(10.5 ounce) can fat-free candensed cream of mushroom soup
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder, or to taste
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • salt and black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Saute ground beef in a large skillet over medium heat untel browned and crumbly(5 - 10 minutes)
  3. Meanwhile, fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to rapid boil
  4. Cook egg noodles at aboil until tender yet firm to the bite(7 - 9 minutes)
  5. Drain and set aside
  6. Drain and discard any fat from the cooked beef
  7. Stir condesned souup and garlic powder into the beef and simmer for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally
  8. Remove beef from heat. Add egg noodles and stir to combine
  9. stir in sour cream and season with salt and pepper
  10. Serve hot and enjoy!
