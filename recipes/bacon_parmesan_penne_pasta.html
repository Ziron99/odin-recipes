Bacon and Parmesan Penne Pasta


Description

This bacon pasta recipe is so easy to make and so flavorful, you'll want to make it a part of your regular dinner routine. It's a great option for bacon lovers and those who want something other than red sauce on thier pasta.


Ingredients

  • 1(16 ounce) package dry penne pasta
  • 1 pound bacon, coarsely chopped
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Bring large pot of lightly salted water to a boil
  2. Add penne and cook, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to bite(about 11 minutes)
  3. While pasta is cooking, cook bacon and onion in a large skillet over medium heat, stirring often, until bacon is crisp and onion is beginning to brown(about 10 minutes)
  4. Remove from the heat and drain grease in small container
  5. Drain pasta and transfer to a large bowl
  6. Add oil and stir to coat pasta
  7. Add cooked bacon and onion plus 1 to 2 tablesppns bacon grease
  8. Sprinkel Parmesan over pasta and stir until well combined.
  9. Add more bacon gease as desired for flavor and moisture
  10. Serve and enjoy!
